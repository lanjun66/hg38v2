Description

Short tandem repeats (STRs), microsatellite density landscapes illustrated the local relative density of local microsatellites per kilobase of sequence along the complete reference Human Genome GRCh38.

This track visualizes the large variation of microsatellite density in local 1-kilobase regions on 24 chromosomes

Methods

The threshold of 6, 3, 3, 3, 3, 3 iterations for mono- to hexa-nucleotide repeat motifs were applied to analyzing microsatellites in the genome. Based on this threshold, the microsatellite density landscapes were mapped.

The ordinate shows the relative microsatellite density values of the local region in 1 kilobase units, and the abscissa shows the specific location of the local region on the chromosome. The local microsatellite relative density pD1RDi = mi / 1kb Ã— 1000 ,mi represents the total number of bases of microsatellite sequences within the ith 1 kilobase unit region on the chromosome
